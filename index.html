Health Prognosis Tool

Welcome to our survival predictor!

If you or a loved one have been diagnosed with a major sickness or are about to undergo a major surgery and are looking for some clarity on your future, this is the tool that you can use to help shed more light on your current situation.

We know that in many cases, doctors give very vague survival chances, often framing things in vague terms like 5-year survival rates which are general and confusing, or stating that they do not know. They do this for a reason, fearing lawsuits and confrontation from the patient and their families, or sometimes, they simply don’t know as every patient is unique and their case different.

However, this tool lets you know exactly what percentage of survival you have given your exact conditions based on existing patient data — with no qualms or misgivings about fear of being sued, since it is all technically just math in the background. Keep in mind that this is based on a machine learning model, and that computers do make mistakes as well.

The dataset used to train this model comes from KoreanVitalDB — nearly 10,000 patients in this dataset were diagnosed with diseases ranging from kidney stones, wounds, to cancer, so there is a very reliable sample size and diversity of data.
